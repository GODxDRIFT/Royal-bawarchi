Our Story

Where Every Dish Tells a Story

At The Royal Bawarchi, we don't just cook food — we craft memories. Nestled in the heart of New Delhi, our kitchen has been a labour of love since 2010, blending age-old family recipes with bold, unapologetic flavours.

From the smoky depth of our Tandoor to the slow-simmered richness of our Dal Makhani, every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the soul of authentic Indian cooking. Our bawarchi (master chef) has spent decades perfecting each spice ratio, each cooking technique — so you get perfection, every single time.

More than a restaurant, we are a gathering place — where families celebrate, friends reconnect, and strangers leave as regulars. Come hungry. Leave happy. That's the Royal Bawarchi promise.
